Experience

The shareholders bring a wealth of experience in managing and overseeing various aspects of international and local infrastructure projects, particularly in the field of water and wastewater systems.

With a combined experience of over 48 years, they have successfully navigated the intricacies of projects falling under the umbrella of the Department of European Union Financial Assistance at the Ministry of Environment, Urbanization, and Climate Change. These projects have spanned critical infrastructure areas such as wastewater treatment plants, drinking water treatment facilities, wastewater collector lines, wastewater pumping stations, drinking water transmission networks, solid waste landfill facilities, and more.

In their roles, they have served as contract managers for projects governed by FIDIC contract terms, ensuring that projects adhere to contractual obligations. Simultaneously, they have overseen supervision contracts, aligning them with PRAG contract conditions for various infrastructure projects.

Their extensive international project experience includes Terms of Reference (ToR) and Tender Documents preparation, particularly in accordance with PRAG procedures. They have played pivotal roles in designing, constructing, starting up, and operating municipal wastewater treatment plants. Throughout their careers, they have demonstrated proficiency in coordinating multiple disciplines during the procurement, design, and construction phases of wastewater treatment plants, wastewater collector lines, wastewater pumping stations, and associated infrastructures.

Additionally, they possess comprehensive expertise in procurement for both international and local projects, covering tasks such as the preparation of tender documents, ToR, and administrative and technical evaluation of tenders. They have served as evaluators, chairpersons, secretaries, and observers in various procurement processes for infrastructure and technical assistance projects.

Furthermore, they have contributed as mechanical and procurement experts in projects aimed at preparing Employer's Requirements and Technical Specifications for wastewater initiatives within the scope of Pre-Accession for the Regional Development Component in Turkey.

Their collective knowledge and experience extend to the preparation of master plans and feasibility studies for integrated water and wastewater projects. This includes shaping the structure of reports, compiling long lists for necessary investments, screening projects for shortlists, conducting prioritization exercises to define the scope of IPA packages, and designing both wastewater and water treatment plants along with conceptual infrastructure. They are well-versed in preparing bill of quantities for these facilities and crafting comprehensive tender documents for all shortlisted components.

Moreover, they excel in project management, collaborating across disciplines during the design and construction phases of wastewater treatment plants, sewerage project components, and related infrastructures. Their experience encompasses proposal preparation, tender document creation, participation in the evaluation stage, and project supervision for a wide array of infrastructure projects.

Beyond project initiation and construction, they are adept at overseeing the startup and operation of municipal wastewater treatment plants of varying scales. They have developed operation and maintenance manuals, conducted staff training for municipal teams, and prepared work certificates, ensuring the smooth functioning of critical facilities.

In summary, the shareholders' combined experience represents a unique blend of technical expertise, project management proficiency, and a deep understanding of international and local infrastructure projects, making them valuable assets in the field of environmental engineering and project implementation.
